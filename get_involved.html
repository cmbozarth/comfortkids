Get Involved!

Helpers of all skill levels are welcome to make Comfort Kids!

From stuffing dolls, cutting fabric, sewing and embroidering, there are several ways you can get involved.


Stuffing

Every so often, we host Stuffing Parties where we stuff dolls and cut fabric. Stuffing and cutting are great ways to contribute if you don't own a sewing machine or don't know how to sew.


Sewing

We are always in need of people with sewing machines for making clothes and dolls. Dolls also need to be hand-sewn and their faces need to be embroidered. Contact us for patterns and ideas and be creative! Please ensure these dolls are made in an allergen and pest-free environment, and please refrain from using buttons or other choking hazards.


Donating

We accept monetary donations and supply donations. We ask that fabrics and supplies are new, unused, and appropriate for the dolls.



The health and safety of those receiving the dolls are the highest priority, so please ensure that the dolls are clean and free from any pests, allergens, or other health hazards.
