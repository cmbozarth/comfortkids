Space Pajamas!


We experimented with clothing patterns and fabric to make these cute space pajamas!


Combining the pants pattern with the shirt pattern and shortening the collar makes a space-man style onesie for the dolls. We chose space fabric, and these kids look like they're ready to go to the moon!


Use appliques to spice up the clothes. A friendly lion can turn the doll into a zookeeper, or a little flower can make a lil' gardener. These space appliques were hand-embroidered, and all it takes to make a planet is a simple circle and a line.


Make use of fun fabrics! See what you can do with colors or themes. You can give the doll pajamas, a profession, or a hobby, or you can simply make it look adorable.
