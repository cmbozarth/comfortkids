About Comfort Kids

Comfort Kids are 100% handmade dolls designed to be huggable and cute. They are donated to neglected children transitioning into foster care in need of a hug and a friendly face.

Want to help?

We are always looking for helpers, whether it be for creating outfits, sewing dolls, embroidering faces, or even just helping us stuff. Learn more about how you can get involved in our doll ministry.
