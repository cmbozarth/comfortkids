About Comfort Kids

Who are Comfort Kids?

Comfort Kids are 100% handmade dolls designed to be huggable and cute. They are made to be unique and contemporary for kids to hug, play with, and be comforted.

Comfort Kids go to children who are abruptly taken from their homes for health and safety reasons. The children may be unable to bring anything with them because of bed bugs or hygienic issues, and this transition to a new home can be scary. Comfort Kids seek to help ease this transition and bring hope to the children while they adjust to their new home.







Comfort Kids started in January 2019 by Cynthia and Julia Bozarth as a means to get community service hours for a class. The two of them soon found that they enjoyed sewing and making things for the benefit of children and the community, so they decided to form Comfort Kids.
